Free Rider HD Timeline
2013–2026

Hi there! Welcome to the Official Free Rider HD Timeline run by TPlacella, the best place to review the history of FRHD, refresh your memory on certain dates, find important threads and most importantly, give access to the game's greatest events and achievements through its 12+ year existence!

This timeline replaces the outdated version by cctvcctvcctv made in 2018. This new timeline now features events from 2021–2026 when cctv's timeline stopped receiving updates. 1/4 of this timeline was done by cctv so praise him for his work in the past. My main goal here is to continue the work of others in the old timeline, fix incorrect dates, add missing information from previous years and most of all, add a wider range of comprehensive sources and information for the community's benefit!

How to navigate the timeline

  • Click on the year you want to search in the sidebar on the left.
  • Press "Ctrl + F" and type in keywords/dates to find your desired information.
  • ((name)) at the end of entries means that the person contributed to a date on the timeline (see Contributors List).
  • It's ok if you get lost. The timeline is easy to read manually; perhaps that's how you want to scroll through the timeline!
  • HAPPY SEARCHING!
