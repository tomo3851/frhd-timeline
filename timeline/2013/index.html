2013

October

  • 18/10

    Free Rider HD Facebook and Free Rider HD Twitter pages created.

  • 26/10

    Kano Apps YouTube Channel created.

November

  • 05/11

    Free Rider HD is created — both the game and forums.

  • 05/11

    First Forum Member is kanoadmin and more Administrators/Moderators join: jschnare and Char (Game Developer).

  • 11/11

    FRHD is linked in WebGames Community on Reddit, spreading the word of the game's creation.

  • 14/11

    Velksy, another Administrator/Moderator, joins the Forums.

  • 19/11

    Community Classic Tracks posted. Wild West by weewam is the first track on the Free Rider HD website, starting from track ID 1001. Tracks are imported from Canvas Rider and posted on developer-made accounts of those authors. Each track is titled '[TRACK NAME] is a Free Rider Community Classic Track by [AUTHOR]' in the description. Community Classic Tracks are contained within track IDs 1001 and 11095, meaning 10,095 Community Classic Tracks were posted. Players could ask on forums to gain access to these developer-made accounts with all their old tracks upon public release.

  • 21–22/11

    More Kano members and admins join forums: Eric, mi7ch, Ira (aka Ira Willey, creator of OneMoreLevel.com), timteh, Griffin, Edmund and kano_admin (pixelsplitter). Members 13–16 are unknown.

  • 22/11

    The first forum thread and message is made in "What is your favourite track?" by Ira.

  • 24/11

    Char posts the first Forum/Game Update about how to get the best performance on FRHD.

  • 28/11

    Char posts an update on how to Register to Forums before the game and forums are opened to the public.

December

  • 01/12

    Experience FRHD like it was before the official release. You can play Epic NthShore by moose_man captured on the Wayback Machine on the 1st of December, 2013. On the leaderboard and in the comments section, you can see Devs and Admins testing out the game. ((NotVeryGood))

  • 04/12

    Free Rider HD Forums is now open to the public. WheelieMaker (weem) is the first normal member on forums (member #17). niclauzon makes the first post by a normal member (post #14). niclauzon also makes the first forum thread by a normal member (thread #10).

  • 05/12

    Free Rider HD is officially released.

  • 05/12

    Eric Welcomes the Community whilst talking about the current state of the game upon its release and future updates.

  • 08/12

    Anything and Everything Not Free Rider topic forum added by Eric after pssst suggested it.

  • 11/12

    Notifications released in-game for when your time is beaten on a track leaderboard.

  • 13/12

    Chrome Web App for FRHD is released (only available on ChromeOS).

  • 15/12

    Post Your WIP Screenshot Here started by Coffee — the most popular and well-known forum thread ever made.

  • 16/12

    Avatar and Sign-up methods released relating to Gravatar, Google+, Facebook and regular emails.

  • 18/12

    Race/Ghost Links are now functional.

  • 20/12

    IRL Pictures thread started by Hashplant.
