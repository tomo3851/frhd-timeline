2014

January

  • 09/01

    Free Rider HD YouTube Channel created.

  • 09/01

    First Free Rider HD Trailer released on YouTube.

  • 15/01

    Eric releases a basic roadmap of what FRHD is going to look like in the coming year.

  • 16/01

    Track Creator Wireframes released by Eric, outlining what the Editor will look like.

  • 17/01

    Official Free Rider HD Subreddit is created.

  • 28/01

    Eric releases an overview of up-and-coming updates and possible ideas for the game in the future.

February

  • 05/02

    FRHD Editor Released as an Alpha version for testing. ((pssst))

  • 18/02

    New Track Creator Hot-Keys released by Char.

  • 19/02

    Mobile Control Improvements proposed by Eric.

  • 21/02

    Best Time on a track added, appears between Timer and Goals. Announcement here by Char.

  • 27/02

    Replay Bug is discovered, announcement by Eric.

March

  • 11/03

    Phone/Tablet + Desktop Updates released by Eric.

  • 14/03

    Track Editor now works for iPads and Tablets. Announcement here by Eric.

  • 26/03

    Points System added into the game for completing a track for the first time.

April

  • 04/04

    Daily Achievements added to the game, 4 initially, giving players coins for future features in the game.

May

  • 08/05

    Everyone is able to upload tracks for the first time. Announcement here by Eric. Tracks cost 250 coins to publish to reduce the amount of tracks each player creates.

  • 08/05

    Char publishes the first track ever made in the Free Rider HD Editor, The Great Volcano. Track ID skips over from 11095 to 50001 so new tracks from the community can be published for the first time. Announcement here by Char.

  • 08/05

    The short drop by Dolphin5 is the first track published by a normal player on Free Rider HD (2nd track ever).

  • 13/05

    New Forum Rules released by Velksy.

  • 13/05

    New Daily Achievement released — the 5th one: "The Critic" — Rate 10 tracks to earn 10 coins.

  • 14/05

    Points are now visible on player profiles. Announcement here by Eric.

  • 15/05

    First Campaign Contest announced by Velksy.

  • 17/05

    Campaign Track Requirements released by Eric: Track 1, Track 2, Track 3, Track 4, Track 5, Track 6, and Track 7.

  • 21/05

    Rest of Campaign Track Requirements released by Eric: Track 8, Track 9 and Track 10.

  • 25/05

    Forum Outage for over 10 hours.

  • 27/05

    New Left Menu Update released by Eric.

  • 29/05

    Friends List Information released by Eric.

June

  • 05/06

    Friends List added to the game, the current max limit being 25 friends.

  • 09/06

    Small update released by Eric. The best time for a track is now shown in the bottom right of the track thumbnail (known to be inaccurate). "Classic Author" icon added to certain player's profiles, allowing players to publish tracks without spending coins.

  • 11/06

    Forum Rules Update — Promotion of Unofficial Free Rider Communities released by Eric.

  • 13/06

    Players can now Challenge Friends in-game.

  • 18/06

    Mid-June Update/Plans released by Eric.

  • 18/06

    Editor Update released by Char about eraser sizes, curved lines and snap-to feature.

  • 29/06

    Blaze06h discovers unused smileys from XenForo that is used for forums. Velksy decides to add a few more as default, but not all of them to prevent the smiley bar from overcrowding. Thread here.

July

  • 04/07

    Free Rider HD Fandom Wiki started.

  • 08/07

    Forum Update (new version and new servers).

  • 10/07

    Ultimate Guide to Track Design released by Max007x.

  • 12/07

    Free Rider Open Campaign Contest started by Velksy for Campaigns 2 and 3.

  • 14/07

    New Sounds released for the bike and deaths.

  • 17/07

    First Campaign released — "Learning the Ropes". Each campaign to follow contains 10 tracks. Unlocking each campaign requires the player to reach a number of medals from the campaigns and have a number of friends. ((Pityboy))

  • 22/07

    Free Rider HD Campaign Gameplay Trailer released on YouTube.

  • 24/07

    Race Colours and Times updated released by Char, indicating who you are ghosting and when someone completes a ghost in a race. Players can also race up to 10 people from the track leaderboard now.

  • 25/07

    Track BB Code added by Char, the best way to show the thumbnail of tracks by ID on forums which also takes you to the track.

  • 29/07

    Curve tool and new zoom settings released by Char.

  • 31/07

    New Daily Achievement — the 6th one: "Warmin' Up" — Complete 5 tracks ("Complete 1 track" is renamed to "Start Your Engine").

  • 31/07

    Summer Series started by speedy__XD, the first big ghosting contest in FRHD.

August

  • 01/08

    Summer Series Round 1 starts. Track here.

  • 06/08

    Velksy shifts from full-time employment to part-time for Kano Apps. mi7ch and Aardvark take over some of the responsibilities. Announcement here.

  • 08/08

    Summer Series Round 1 ends. Round 2 starts.

  • 15/08

    Summer Series Round 2 ends. Round 3 starts.

  • 21/08

    Campaign #2 Training Wheels Off is released. Announcement here by Eric.

  • 22/08

    Summer Series Round 3 ends. Round 4 starts.

  • 27/08

    Official FRHD Trailer released on YouTube, thus beginning the rise of Free Rider HD and the rapid growth of the Free Rider HD Community.

  • 28/08

    Player and Friend Lifetime Leaderboards for points released by Eric (only shows those over 1,000 points).

  • 29/08

    Summer Series Round 4 ends. Round 5 starts.

  • 30/08

    Author Leaderboard for upvotes on tracks released by Eric (only shows those with over 200 likes).

September

  • 05/09

    Open Campaign Contest started by Eric to get an influx of tracks for proposed future Campaigns.

  • 05/09

    Summer Series ends, final results released.

  • 06/09

    Embed Free Rider Tracks feature announced by Eric, allowing players to play FRHD tracks on forums and other websites.

  • 15/09

    Classic Author badge on player profiles changed to Official Author. Discussion here.

  • 17/09

    FRHD News created by FREEZ and by Cryogenic ((Volund and Cryogenic))

  • 20/09

    Drawing Thread started by Maple.

  • 31/09

    Top Rated page added to the game and the cost of uploading tracks for people without Official Author reduced from 250 to 50. Announcement here by Eric.

October

  • 02/10

    All FRHD News Issues are compiled into one thread (now known as Series 1).

  • 04/10

    FRHD is top-voted on the AndroidGaming Reddit. Announcement here by Eric.

  • 10/10

    Track Editor Uploading slightly changed, requiring regular players to complete the track and not complete the track in 0 seconds before publishing.

  • 22/10

    Automatic Best Time Ghost featured added by Char so whenever you load into a track you've already completed, it shows your current best ghost so you can race against it.

  • 31/10

    Campaign #3 Action is released. Announcement here by Eric.

  • 31/10

    Forum Monthly Post Leaderboard added. Whoever has the most posts in a month is displayed on the "Previous Month Winner" tab, with the current top 10 members with the most points in that month below it on the right side of the forum's main page. The current member with the most points in a month receives the "Top Poster of the Month" banner on forums.

    Profiles now show ghosts and headgear. Random tracks slightly reworked. Announcement here by Eric.
