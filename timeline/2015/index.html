June

  • 14/06

    After exactly 6 months, with over 1000 pages and 20,000 posts, pssst thread is finally deleted by Eric due to excessive spam and content not following the forum's PG-13 guidelines. Players begged Eric to archive the thread as it was "a piece of history" on the forums, but due to complaints from parents about what kids saw, Eric's best option was to delete the thread entirely rather than painfully delete every inappropriate message. pssst created Rip pssst thread to remember this moment in FRHD History. Some people had waited for this to happen for months, while others wanted the pssst thread to last forever. Nonetheless, a new era in forum activity had just begun.
