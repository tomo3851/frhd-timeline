    Daily Track is added. Moderators select trending tracks or high-quality old tracks to feature on Daily Track for 7 days. 7 tracks are on the list at a time and it refreshes every 24 hours, adding and removing a track from the list rotation style. Daily tracks are determined by moderators weeks in advance, and sometimes in rare cases, there have been no daily tracks.
