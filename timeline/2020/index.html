    RedOrBlue discovers a ghost by clockwe2 on his track Difficult that allows him to go back through a teleport and gain extra time for the helicopter powerup when pressing "enter" to go back to a checkpoint. An interaction like this has never been seen before, requiring precision when just picking up the powerup and going back to the checkpoint. The discovery is coined "the vehicle glitch".

  • 04/07

    Heli Tech by Coated_Badger posted, putting the new vehicle glitch to the test on a new track. It is also discovered that you can get infinite time on a vehicle powerup with the same method clockwe2 used but you don't need a teleport and just a checkpoint. This version of the glitch is much harder to pull off and is almost frame perfect.

By 26/11/2019, it had become somewhat more respected, with a handful of other users also sharing the role. In the end, the role was deemed to make certain people feel official and to prevent begging for power/roles, therefore being ultimately useless.
